about
Studio Wenden is a design practice layering architectural volumes into richly sensorial spaces.

Grounded in Darlinghurst, Australia, the studio is led by Amy Wenden, whose work blends interior design, product design, art direction and styling to foreground the human experience. Distinctive in atmosphere, the ensuing spaces are characterised by a deep engagement with locality and a timeless sense of belonging.
‍
Accompanying the design studio is a curated gallery of rare and vintage objects. Selected for their distinctive presence, every piece in the slowly evolving supply is available for purchase or hire.
