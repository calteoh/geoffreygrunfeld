about
Studio Wenden is a design practice based in Sydney, Australia. Led by Amy Wenden, she collaborates with brands globally across the hospitality, residential and furniture manufacturing sectors.

Services are tailored to each client and vary from creative direction, brand strategy, interior design and styling. Common to all projects is a commitment to creating spaces that are human and relevant, and building client relationships that are long lasting.

Prior to launching her studio, Amy worked as a Design Director in London. There she led a design strategy team and worked with international brands in the hospitality, retail, luxury lifestyle, start up, FMCG and financial sectors. Collaborations included Hyundai Card, Mandarin Oriental, Hyatt Regency, Ritz Carlton, Four Seasons, Marriott, Westfield and WWRD. In most recent years she has been collaborating with Lambs and Lions, Berlin as an interior stylist.
